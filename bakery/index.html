A little history about us

since 2015 The Little Bakery started out very little at a little town called Lilville, located at the heart of Atlanta. Our founder, Pequeno Little, put in years of hard work to come up with the perfect dough recipe that satisfied Atlantans and people all across America.

Now, we ship our freshly baked bread all around the globe. In short, we turned into a multi-million dollar frozen food company. Not so little now eh?
